SHIPPING POLICY — GULF REGION

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

1. Delivery Coverage

COSIMA delivers its beauty and elegance products across all six Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries:

  • United Arab Emirates (UAE)
  • Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA)
  • Kuwait
  • Qatar
  • Bahrain
  • Sultanate of Oman

We partner with trusted international and regional logistics providers to ensure reliable nationwide coverage within each country.

2. Order Processing Times

All orders are processed within 24 to 48 business hours after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next available business day.

You will receive an order confirmation email immediately after your purchase, followed by a shipping confirmation once your order has been dispatched.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking number. You can use this number to monitor your delivery status directly on our logistics partner's website.

For any tracking issues or inquiries, please contact our customer service team using the details provided at the end of this policy.

6. Receiving Your Order

Upon receiving your package, we recommend the following:

  • Inspect the packaging for any visible damage before accepting delivery.
  • Verify the contents match your order.
  • Report any damage, missing items, or discrepancies to the delivery agent immediately and notify COSIMA within 24 hours of receipt.

For issues with your order, please refer to our Returns & Exchange Policy for further guidance.

7. Customs & Import Duties

Orders shipped to GCC countries may be subject to local customs duties, taxes, or import fees upon arrival. These charges are determined by the destination country's customs authority and are the sole responsibility of the customer.

COSIMA is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing. We recommend checking your country's import regulations for beauty and cosmetic products before placing your order.

8. Failed or Missed Delivery

In the event of a failed delivery attempt, the carrier will typically:

  • Make a second delivery attempt on the following business day.
  • Leave a delivery notification with instructions for collection from a local depot or pickup point.

If a package is returned to us due to repeated failed delivery attempts or an incorrect address provided by the customer, re-shipping fees will be charged to the customer.
