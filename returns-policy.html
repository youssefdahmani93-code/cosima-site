RETURNS & EXCHANGE POLICY — GULF REGION

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

At COSIMA, your satisfaction is our priority. We understand that occasionally a product may not meet your expectations. This policy outlines the conditions and procedures for returns and exchanges for orders placed on www.cosima.beauty and delivered across the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region.

1. Return & Exchange Window

You have 14 calendar days from the date of delivery to request a return or exchange. Requests submitted after this period will not be accepted.

To be eligible, items must meet all of the following conditions:

  • The product is unused and in its original, sealed condition.
  • The original packaging, labels, and accessories are intact.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or invoice) is provided.
  • The request is submitted within 14 days of the confirmed delivery date.

3. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Opened or used beauty, skincare, or cosmetic products.
  • Products without original packaging or missing seals.
  • Items purchased during final sale or promotional events (clearly marked at time of purchase).
  • Gift cards or vouchers.

These restrictions are in place to protect the health and safety of all our customers and are in line with GCC consumer protection standards.

4. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please notify us within 24 hours of delivery. To process your claim, we will require:

  • Clear photographs of the damaged or incorrect item.
  • A photo of the outer packaging showing any visible damage.
  • Your order number and a brief description of the issue.

COSIMA will cover all return shipping costs and arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional charge to you.

Do not return items without a valid RMA number. Unauthorized returns will not be accepted and may be returned to sender at the customer's expense.

6. Return Shipping

Unless the return is due to a COSIMA error (wrong or defective item), return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer. We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure safe return of the product.

COSIMA is not responsible for items lost or damaged during return transit.

7. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you by email of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Approved refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refunded to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.
  • Bank Transfer: Processed within 7–14 business days depending on your bank.
  • Store Credit: Available immediately upon approval and valid for 12 months.

Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

8. Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different variant (shade, size, or formulation), please indicate your preference when submitting your return request. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

If your requested exchange item is unavailable, we will offer you the choice of a full refund or store credit.

9. GCC Consumer Rights

This policy is designed to comply with consumer protection regulations across GCC member states. Customers retain all statutory rights afforded under local laws. Nothing in this policy limits or excludes your legal rights as a consumer.

  • UAE: Federal Law No. 15 of 2020 on Consumer Protection.
  • KSA: Consumer Protection Law issued by Royal Decree M/25.
  • Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman: Applicable national consumer protection legislation.
